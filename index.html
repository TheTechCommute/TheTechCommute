"Often advice I give to new employees when they would join at Netflix was don't hold back, like as questions, like give ideas, things like that. I think early days I just was just in this observing mode and would sometimes like see room for some improvements or questioning of something, and sometimes I would hold back because I would think, they probably already thought of this. But I realized quickly that no that's a part of my job to poke on the ideas and really think about them strategically, ask the dumb questions that you think they have thought of that often that haven't"

"You need to take more breaks than you think you might need to. When you are learning, if they are actually learning, it should be exhausting. - Kent C Dodds
